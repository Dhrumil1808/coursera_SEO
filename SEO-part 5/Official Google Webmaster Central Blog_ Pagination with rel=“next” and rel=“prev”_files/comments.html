Canonical is not required. They are independent signals. I haven't seen any issues with still including a self-referencing canonical tag on each page of the series. Canonicalizing those pages back to a non-view all state would send a conflicting signal to the search engines and potentially cause the rel next/prev to be ignored.﻿
